# DealsNest - Affiliate Marketing Platform

## Overview
DealsNest is a live affiliate marketing platform where you can:
- 📱 Add and manage affiliate links from multiple e-commerce platforms
- 🎯 Share deals and earn commissions when users click and buy
- 💰 Track clicks, conversions, and estimated earnings
- 📊 Monitor performance with a real-time dashboard

## Features

### 1. Add Affiliate Links
- Easy-to-use form to add new deals
- Support for multiple platforms: Amazon, eBay, Flipkart, AliExpress
- Add custom commission rates
- Include product images and descriptions
- Track original and discounted prices

### 2. Browse Deals
- View all active affiliate deals
- Filter by platform or category
- Search for specific products
- Click through to earn commissions

### 3. Dashboard Analytics
- Real-time tracking of clicks
- Monitor conversions
- Track estimated earnings
- Performance breakdown by deal

### 4. Local Storage
- All data is saved locally in your browser
- No backend required
- Fully offline capable

## How to Use

### Step 1: Add a Deal
1. Click "Add Link" in the navigation
2. Fill in the deal details:
   - Title and description
   - Select platform and category
   - Enter original and discounted prices
   - Paste your affiliate link
   - Set commission rate
   - Add product image URL
3. Click "Add Deal"

### Step 2: Monitor Performance
1. Go to "Dashboard"
2. View your stats:
   - Total clicks
   - Active deals
   - Estimated earnings
3. Check performance table for detailed breakdown

### Step 3: Share & Earn
1. Share deals from your platform
2. Earn commission when users click and purchase
3. Track everything in real-time

## Commission Tracking
The platform automatically calculates estimated earnings based on:
- Number of clicks on your affiliate link
- Commission rate percentage
- Product price

Formula: `(Clicks × Commission%) × (Price / 10)`

## Tips for Success

1. **Quality Deals**
   - Focus on genuine discounts
   - Write compelling descriptions
   - Use clear product images

2. **Optimize Commission Rates**
   - Research typical rates on each platform
   - Higher rates attract more clicks
   - Be realistic with percentages

3. **Promote Effectively**
   - Share deals on social media
   - Use engaging titles
   - Update deals regularly

4. **Track Performance**
   - Check dashboard regularly
   - Remove low-performing deals
   - Double down on winners
