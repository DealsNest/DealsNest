# 🔗 How to Get Working Affiliate Links

## Amazon Affiliate Links

### Step 1: Sign Up for Amazon Associates
1. Go to https://associate-program.amazon.com/
2. Click "Join Now for Free"
3. Sign in with your Amazon account
4. Complete the registration and application

### Step 2: Get Your Associate ID
1. Once approved, go to your account settings
2. Your **Associate ID** is in the Account Settings page
3. It looks like: `yourname-20`

### Step 3: Create Affiliate Links

#### Method 1: Using Amazon's Link Generator (Recommended)
1. Log in to https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/
2. Click "Product Links" → "Link Generator"
3. Search for any product
4. Copy the generated link
5. Example format: `https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0XXXXXXXXX?tag=yourname-20`

#### Method 2: Manual Link Format
Replace `yourname-20` with your Associate ID:
```
https://www.amazon.com/dp/PRODUCT_ID?tag=yourname-20
```

#### Method 3: Search Links
```
https://www.amazon.com/s?k=search+term&tag=yourname-20
```

### ✅ Working Example Link Format
```
https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08EXAMPLE?tag=yourname-20
```

## eBay Affiliate Links

### Step 1: Join eBay Partner Network
1. Go to https://www.ebaypartnernetwork.com/
2. Click "Register"
3. Complete signup

### Step 2: Get Campaign ID
1. In your account, go to Tools
2. Find your **Campaign ID**
3. Copy it

### Step 3: Create Links
Format:
```
https://ebay.com/itm/PRODUCT_ID?campid=YOUR_CAMPAIGN_ID
```

---

## Flipkart Affiliate Links

### Step 1: Join Flipkart Affiliate
1. Go to https://www.flipkart.com/affiliate
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Complete registration

### Step 2: Get Your Link
1. Search for a product on Flipkart
2. Copy the product URL
3. Format: `https://fktr.in/XXXXX` or direct product link

### ✅ Working Example
```
https://www.flipkart.com/p/PRODUCT_ID?affid=YOUR_AFFILIATE_ID
```

---

## AliExpress Affiliate Links

### Step 1: Join AliExpress Affiliate
1. Go to https://portals.aliexpress.com/
2. Sign up as seller
3. Go to Affiliate Program section

### Step 2: Get Your Link
1. Search product on AliExpress
2. Use their link generator
3. Format: `https://s.click.aliexpress.com/YOUR_LINK_CODE`

## How to Add to DealsNest

Once you have a **working affiliate link**:

1. Open DealsNest platform (index.html)
2. Click **"Add Link"** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Deal Title**: Product name
   - **Description**: What makes it a good deal
   - **Platform**: Amazon, eBay, etc.
   - **Original Price**: Normal price
   - **Discounted Price**: Sale price
   - **Affiliate Link**: Your affiliate link (e.g., https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08EXAMPLE?tag=yourname-20)
   - **Commission Rate**: Check your affiliate dashboard (typically 3-5%)
   - **Image URL**: Product image link

4. Click **"Add Deal"**

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- ❌ Using short links without your affiliate ID/tag
- ❌ Using expired links
- ❌ Links without proper encoding
- ❌ Copying links with tracking that doesn't belong to you

✅ **Do This:**
- ✓ Always include your affiliate ID/tag in the link
- ✓ Test the link before adding
- ✓ Use official link generators from each platform
- ✓ Keep your affiliate account active

## Testing Your Links

1. Add the link to DealsNest
2. Click "View Deal" 
3. Make sure it opens the correct product page
4. Check that your affiliate ID is in the URL
